Welcome to beta.USA.gov, the first step to a new USA.gov.

Our mission has always been to make it easy for you to find and understand government information and services. With this test site, we're developing new ways to do that even better.

On beta.USA.gov, you'll find simplified pages, with more focused answers to your questions, and new, interactive tools. Right now the beta site is small, with limited links and a handful of popular topics. But we'll keep adding more until beta.USA.gov covers all the important topics you need. And if you're looking for a topic that isn't on this test site yet, we can still help. Just visit the current version of USA.gov to get the information you need.
